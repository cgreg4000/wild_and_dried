About Wild and Dried

Wild and Dried Co exists to create sustainable, natural, beautiful dried floral arrangements, bouquet, gifts, and accessories that will last forever! Wild and Dried Co was created out a season of transition in my life. There were some key things that had been missing from my every day; creativity, freedom, and flexibility. Wild and Dried Co allows me all of those things and my hope is that i can bring those things into your home and world.
